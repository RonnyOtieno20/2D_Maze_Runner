MazeRunner

Navigate through procedurally generated mazes in this exciting command-line adventure!

View on GitHub

Key Features

  • Procedural Maze Generation: Each game presents a unique maze challenge, ensuring no two playthroughs are the same.
  • ASCII Rendering: Experience the maze in beautifully crafted ASCII art, bringing classic charm to modern screens.
  • Vim-style Navigation: Use the classic h, j, k, l keys for efficient movement through the maze, a nod to the beloved text editor.
  • Scoring System: Challenge yourself to reach the exit in the fewest moves and shortest time possible, adding a competitive edge to your maze-solving skills.
  • Save and Load: Pause your adventure and resume it later, perfect for when you need a break from your maze-solving quest.

About the Project

MazeRunner was born from my fascination with procedural generation and my nostalgia for text-based games. This project, developed as part of my portfolio for Holberton School, pushed me to explore advanced algorithms and create an engaging user experience within the constraints of a command-line interface.
